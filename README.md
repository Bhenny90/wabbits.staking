## Ecosystem Overview

Energy Wabbits is built around a simple but rewarding loop:

- Stake your Energy Wabbits
- Earn EP every hour
- Use EP to power and upgrade your Burrow Stop
- Generate WB daily
- Spend WB in the Burrow Shop, Burrow Casino, and Wabbit Raffles
- Build your Collab Score through activity and consistency
- Unlock community positioning, rankings, and perks

## Main Currencies

### EP (Energy Points)
EP is the in-game ecosystem currency earned by staking Wabbits. It is used for upgrades, maintenance, staffing, and casino chip conversion.

### WB (Wabbit Bucks)
WB is the daily reward currency generated by your Burrow Stop. It can be used for redemptions, raffles, and casino activity.

### SOL
SOL is used for select premium actions such as daily WB claims, boosts, shop redemption fees, and certain casino cashouts.

## Key Features

- NFT staking

## Important Notes

- Max WB hold is **25 WB**
- If your WB storage is full, excess WB is wasted
- Maintenance must be paid every 24 hours to preserve efficiency
- Boosts increase EP production for 24 hours
- Activity contributes to your Collab Score and overall ecosystem standing
