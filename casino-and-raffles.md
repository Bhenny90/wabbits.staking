# 🎰 Casino, Arcade, and Raffles

The Energy Wabbits ecosystem includes multiple ways for users to put their earned value to use.

These systems are designed to increase engagement, create excitement, and give WB and EP more utility.

---

## Burrow Casino

The Burrow Casino allows players to convert WB or EP into chips and use those chips in gameplay systems.

### Available Casino Games
- Slots
- Roulette
- Coinflip

### Core Rules
- Playing games is free
- Chips are the gameplay currency
- Users can convert EP or WB into chips
- Different cashout paths include different fees

### EP to Chips
EP converts to chips at a 1:1 rate.

A 10% share of EP goes to the jackpot pool, helping sustain prize funding and larger ecosystem excitement.

### WB to Chips
WB also converts to chips at a 1:1 rate with no additional fee.

This gives players a direct way to use their daily earned WB inside the casino.

### Cashouts
Two main cashout routes exist:

#### Chips to WB
- ~$0.25 SOL fee
- 10% chip fee

#### Chips to Real-Value EP Path
- $1.00 SOL fee
- Subject to daily and weekly limits

### Why Casino Fees Exist
Casino fees help:
- Support treasury growth
- Fund jackpot mechanics
- Sustain ecosystem rewards
- Limit abuse
- Create a healthy gameplay economy

---

## Arcade

The Arcade is a separate gameplay lane from the casino.

### Arcade Rules
- All arcade games are free
- Designed for fun, engagement, and retention
- Adds more value to the ecosystem without creating constant spend pressure

The Arcade exists to keep the ecosystem entertaining even outside of reward-heavy systems.

---

## Wabbit Raffles

Wabbit Raffles allow players to spend WB for chances to win prizes.

### Raffle Rules
- Tickets are purchased using WB only
- No SOL fee
- Ticket pricing is set per raffle by admin
- Prize structure can vary by event

### Why Raffles Matter
Raffles give WB another strong use case and create:
- Community excitement
- Prize-driven participation
- A reason to stay active and keep earning WB
