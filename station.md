# ⛽ The Burrow Stop

The Burrow Stop is the heart of the Energy Wabbits idle progression system.

It turns staking output into a daily gameplay loop by allowing players to spend EP, improve their store, and generate WB.

## Purpose of the Station

The Station is designed to:
- Give EP meaningful utility
- Reward active players over passive inactivity
- Create a daily management loop
- Drive WB generation
- Connect staking to the rest of the ecosystem

## Main Station Tabs

### Collect

The Collect tab is where you purchase and upgrade partner can tiers.

Your first can unlocks the Station.

Higher tiers generate more daily WB.

#### Can Tiers
- Can
- 12-Pack
- 24-Pack

The more advanced your can tier, the stronger your shop output.

---

### Staff

The Staff tab lets you invest EP into crew members.

Crew members power your Station and improve performance.

#### Roles
- Stocker
- Manager
- Night Manager

Each active worker requires one staked Wabbit.

Higher level workers improve:
- Worker score
- Overall shop score
- Efficiency

---

### Stats

The Stats tab provides a live view of your store’s performance.

This section displays:
- Daily WB generation
- Efficiency level
- Maintenance health
- EP production formula

---

### Ranks

The Ranks tab shows ecosystem standings and competitive positioning.

This encourages:
- More activity
- More optimization
- More competition between holders

## Shop Score Formula

Your station output is based on your shop score.

**Shop Score = Partner Cans Score + Workers Score, modified by Efficiency**

This means:
- Better can tiers increase score
- Stronger staff improves score
- Efficiency amplifies total results

## Efficiency

Efficiency determines how well your Station performs.

If you skip maintenance, efficiency drops.

When efficiency drops:
- Daily WB generation weakens
- Station performance declines
- Your overall progression slows down

## Maintenance

Maintenance must be paid every 24 hours in EP.

This keeps your Station operating properly.

### Why Maintenance Exists
- Prevents full passive farming
- Creates a daily return loop
- Gives EP another meaningful use
- Rewards consistency

## WB Capacity

Your Station can only hold up to **25 WB** at a time.

If you do not claim before hitting the cap:
- Your Station stops storing additional WB
- Excess WB is wasted

This creates a daily engagement loop and rewards active participation.
